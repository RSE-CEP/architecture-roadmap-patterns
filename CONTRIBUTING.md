# Contributing to RSE-CEP

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Research Software Engineering Capacity Enhancement Project! We welcome contributions from the HASS and Indigenous research community, RSEs, software engineers, and GLAM sector professionals.

## 🌟 How to Contribute

### Suggesting New Patterns

If you have a research software engineering pattern that would benefit the HASS and Indigenous research community:

1. Review the [Pattern Definition Guide](Patterns/1%20-%20Pattern_Definition_Guide.md) to understand the pattern framework
2. Use the [Pattern Template](Patterns/2%20-%20Pattern_Template.md) to document your pattern
3. Submit a pull request with your proposed pattern
4. Include context about where and how this pattern has been successfully applied

### Improving Existing Content

- **Fix typos or clarify language**: Submit a pull request with your improvements
- **Add examples**: Practical examples are highly valued - share your real-world applications
- **Update outdated information**: Help keep the content current with evolving technologies

### Sharing Architectural Guidance

If you have architectural principles or guidance relevant to HASS and Indigenous research software:

1. Review existing content in the Architecture/ folder
2. Ensure your contribution aligns with research ethics, FAIR principles, and CARE principles
3. Submit with clear documentation and references where applicable

### Contributing to the Roadmap

Technology landscape insights are welcome:
- Emerging technologies relevant to HASS research
- Updates on tool maturity and adoption
- Strategic planning considerations

## 📝 Submission Guidelines

### Pull Requests

1. **Fork** the repository
2. **Create a branch** for your contribution (`git checkout -b feature/new-pattern-name`)
3. **Make your changes** following the existing style and structure
4. **Test** that all links work and formatting is correct
5. **Commit** with clear, descriptive messages
6. **Push** to your fork
7. **Submit a pull request** with a clear description of your contribution

### Writing Style

- Use clear, accessible language
- Define technical terms where appropriate
- Include practical examples
- Cite sources and references
- Be inclusive and respectful of diverse research contexts

### Respect for Indigenous Knowledge

When contributing content related to Indigenous research or data:

- Ensure you have appropriate permissions to share information
- Respect Indigenous data sovereignty principles
- Follow CARE Principles for Indigenous Data Governance
- Acknowledge Traditional Owners and knowledge holders appropriately
- When in doubt, consult with Indigenous research leaders

## 🤝 Code of Conduct

All contributors must adhere to our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment.

## 📧 Questions?

If you have questions about contributing:
- Open an issue for discussion
- Visit the [project homepage](https://ardc.edu.au/project/research-software-engineering-capacity-enhancement-project/)
- Register your interest via the [ARDC HASS and Indigenous Research Data Commons](https://ardc.edu.au/hass-and-indigenous-research-data-commons/)

## 🙏 Recognition

Contributors will be acknowledged in the project. Significant contributions may be highlighted in project reports and communications.

## Review Process

Contributions will be reviewed by the project team to ensure:
- Alignment with project goals
- Quality and accuracy of content
- Appropriate respect for Indigenous knowledge and cultural protocols
- Consistency with existing patterns and structure

We aim to review pull requests within 2-3 weeks, though complex contributions may take longer.

---

Thank you for helping build research software engineering capability in the HASS and Indigenous research community!
